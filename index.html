「 about 」

UK based Audio Analyst for PlayStation Studios QA. Working integrated with PlayStation's Creative Arts Sound team and partner studios to help bringing you critically acclaimed and award-winning titles such as God of War: Ragnarök, Returnal and Sackboy: A Big Adventure.


Cambridge's Anglia Ruskin University alumni with an MSc Computer Games Development and a BSc (Hons) Audio and Music Technology, and previously a longtime Mentor at Brains Eden Gaming Festival, the UK's biggest games jam.
